We are also artists.

We are a team of many fields of expertise. Together we accomplish so much, and we go so far. First and foremost, we know about art. We have our own ceramic business which gathered us together on this project in the first place. We know what ceramists need and we are familiar with the culture.

the world becomes a prettier place

We create web-based services based on the state of the art tech. Together we design, implement the functionality and take care of the transactions. We talk to the blockchain live and in stereo, on demand.
