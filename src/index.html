Why
Choose
Ceramos?

With Ceramos you can have your own website to showcase your art.
Ceramic lovers from all over the world will be able to browse through your exquisite pieces of work and buy them directly from you. You are in control of the pricing, the shipping, and the inventory. Ceramos is a perfect alternative to major payment processing companies as it offers near-zero handling costs and does not deduct taxes or any additional or hidden fees.

At Ceramos we believe that "Simple is the new sexy."
By designing a user-friendly UX, we allow your customers to focus on the beauty of your creation, be fascinated by it, and cherish it. Art is a language of love and so shall remain. We make up for the complexity by providing a legitimately state-of-the-art application protocol to safely handle your transactions.
