This is Amaya!

Amaya is a talented ceramist who creates beautiful art.

Amaya makes all sorts of ceramic sculptures and objects. She makes mugs and dishes. She makes vases with all sorts of beautiful glazes and over-glazes. She's even got her own recipes! Amaya knows what temperature the kiln should be and to what cone the earthenware should be fired.

Her Passion

is her career. She is looking for a gallery.

A gallery allows her work to be seen by thousands of people. A physical gallery would be expensive and would limit the visitors to a specific region.

A Showcase

is what she needs. For all to see.

Well yes. Instagram is the first showcase that comes to mind. But is this the best she can do? What if Amaya had a website in which she could accept orders and payments in an organized way?

And now:

That's where we come in.

We can offer Amaya a website in which she organizes her products and prices them however she wants. Customers would visit her gallery and choose only the finest works. They pay in their money of choice and lay back in their chairs, waiting.

-| Success! |-

She's got money. They've got art.

She has started to sell her art for higher prices and in higher volumes. That's what happens when products are presented nicely. Everyone is happy! The process was fast and enjoyable. They not only enjoyed the art, but the way they purchased it.
